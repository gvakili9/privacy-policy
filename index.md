Privacy Policy for NoteFlash
Last updated: March 23, 2026
Overview
NoteFlash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application NoteFlash ("App") handles your information.
Information We Collect
NoteFlash does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The App processes the notes you enter solely to generate flashcards and does not retain this content after your session ends.
Third-Party Services
The App uses the OpenAI API to process your notes and generate flashcard content. Text you enter is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy at https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy for details on how they handle data.
Subscriptions
Subscription payments are processed through the Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's and Google's privacy policies for details.
Data Storage
NoteFlash stores only your free trial start date locally on your device to manage trial access. No personal data is stored on our servers.
Children's Privacy
NoteFlash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on this page.
