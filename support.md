## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I generate flashcards?**
Paste your notes into the text box and tap Generate Cards.

**How does the free trial work?**
You get 3 days free, then $0.99/month. Cancel anytime.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to your Google Play or App Store subscription settings and cancel anytime.

**The app isn't generating cards. What should I do?**
Make sure you have an internet connection and try again.
